Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

1. Our Commitment

SEFA is committed to maintaining a safe platform and protecting users from harmful, abusive, or illegal behaviour. SEFA is intended only for adults aged 18 and over.

2. Minimum Age Requirement

Users must be at least 18 years old to create an account or use SEFA. We do not knowingly allow children to use the platform.

3. Zero Tolerance for CSAE and CSAM

SEFA has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation, including child sexual abuse material, grooming, sexual solicitation of minors, or any behaviour that harms or exploits children.

4. Prohibited Content and Behaviour

Users may not upload, share, request, promote, or distribute content involving child sexual abuse or exploitation. Users may not use SEFA to contact, groom, exploit, threaten, or endanger minors.

5. Reporting and Review

Users can report concerning content, behaviour, or accounts through in-app reporting tools where available or by contacting us at info@sefa-app.ch. Reports are reviewed and action is taken when required.

6. Enforcement

Accounts or content that violate these standards may be removed, suspended, or permanently banned. Serious cases may be reported to the relevant authorities where appropriate or legally required.

7. Cooperation with Authorities

SEFA may cooperate with law enforcement, child protection organisations, app store providers, and other relevant authorities when necessary to protect children and comply with applicable law.

8. Contact

For child safety concerns, please contact SEFA Support at info@sefa-app.ch. Please include enough information for us to review the issue quickly and safely.
