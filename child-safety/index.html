Child Safety Standards

Last updated: June 2026

SEFA (operated by Westinas, Neugasse 6, 8005 Zürich, Switzerland) is committed to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

Our Commitment

SEFA is an 18+ social event companionship app. We do not knowingly allow minors to register or use the platform. SEFA has zero tolerance for any content or behavior that exploits, endangers, or sexualizes children.

Prevention Measures

  • Age restriction: SEFA's target audience is set to users 18 years and older. Account creation requires agreement to our Terms of Service, which prohibit use by minors.
  • No identity verification of minors as a substitute: We do not collect government ID, in line with Swiss data protection law (revDSG), but we actively monitor for and act on reports of suspected underage use.
  • In-app reporting: Users can report any profile, message, or event listing that raises a child safety concern directly within the app, or by contacting us at the email below.
  • Content review: Reported content is reviewed by our team and removed promptly if it violates these standards. Accounts found violating child safety standards are permanently banned.
  • No private messaging outside event context: Messaging on SEFA (Together plan) is limited to users who have both indicated they are attending the same public event, reducing opportunities for predatory contact.

Reporting a Concern

If you encounter content or behavior on SEFA that you believe endangers a child, or you suspect CSAE material or activity, please contact us immediately:

Email: info@sefa-app.ch
Subject line: "Child Safety Report"

We review all reports promptly and cooperate with law enforcement and relevant authorities (including Swiss authorities and NCMEC where applicable) as required by law.

Policy Compliance

SEFA complies with Google Play's Child Safety Standards Policy and applicable Swiss and international law regarding the protection of minors.
