How it works

Three steps to meeting someone new at the door.

1

Pick an event

Find a concert, festival, theatre show, or more in Zürich — browsing doesn't require an account.

2

Say "I'm going"

Others heading to the same event become visible — see their profiles if you'd like.

3

Meet at the meeting point

Meet up before the event at the agreed meeting point and head in together.

Anonymous by default

At first you only see how many people are going — viewing profiles and chatting are optional.

Meeting point

Before the event, a shared meeting point and status (on the way / running late) is set.

Controlled messaging

On the Together plan, message attendees using template messages — without sharing your phone number or email.
