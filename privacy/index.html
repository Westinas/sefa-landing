SEFA Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SEFA. SEFA is a Social Event Friendship Platform that helps people discover events and connect with others who are interested in attending the same activities.

SEFA focuses on shared experiences around concerts, festivals, sports events, cultural activities, and social gatherings.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

By using SEFA, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name or account name provided by the authentication service
  • Optional profile photo, if you choose to upload one

Activity Information. We may collect information related to:

  • Interests selected within the app
  • Events viewed
  • Events joined
  • Events created
  • Event participation activity

Device and Technical Information. We may collect:

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Application version
  • Diagnostic information
  • Crash reports
  • Usage analytics

Subscription Information. If you subscribe to a premium plan, we may receive information about your subscription status from Apple App Store, Google Play Store, or our subscription management provider. SEFA does not receive or store your full payment card information.

3. Information We Do Not Require

SEFA does not require users to provide:

  • Gender information
  • Age or date of birth
  • Phone numbers
  • Home addresses
  • Government identification documents
  • Biometric information

Users should avoid sharing unnecessary personal information within event descriptions, comments, or conversations.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Provide access to events and social features
  • Display relevant events and recommendations
  • Improve application performance and user experience
  • Maintain platform security
  • Detect fraud, abuse, and policy violations
  • Manage subscriptions and premium features
  • Respond to support requests

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

We may share limited information with trusted service providers that help us operate the platform, including: authentication providers, cloud hosting providers, analytics providers, crash reporting providers, and subscription management providers. These providers may only process information as necessary to provide their services.

We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of SEFA, our users, or others.

6. Third Party Services

SEFA may use third-party services such as Firebase, Google Analytics for Firebase, Firebase Crashlytics, RevenueCat, Apple App Store, and Google Play Store. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services, maintain account functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When information is no longer required, it will be deleted or anonymized where reasonably possible.

8. Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated personal information at any time. To request account deletion, please contact info@sefa-app.ch. Upon verification of the request, we will delete or anonymize personal information unless retention is required by law.

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Children's Privacy

SEFA is intended only for individuals who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that personal information from a person under 18 has been collected, we will take reasonable steps to delete such information.

11. International Users

SEFA may use service providers located in different countries. By using the service, you understand that your information may be processed and stored in countries outside your country of residence, subject to appropriate legal safeguards where required.

12. GDPR and Swiss Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access your data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion of data, restrict processing, object to processing, and data portability where applicable. Requests can be submitted using the contact information below.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted within the application and on our website. Continued use of SEFA after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
